#+title: augmented matrix


The [[id:08dce69d-0252-4201-9f50-e864901fd373][matrix]] in augmented form has the solution to the equation in the
last column.

for example

\(x_1 + 2 x_2 + 8x_3 = 9 \)
\(x_1 + 0x_2 + 1x_3 = 7\)

would be

\(\begin{pmatrix}
1 & 2 & 8 & 9 \\ 1 & 0 & 1 & 7
\end{pmatrix}\)
