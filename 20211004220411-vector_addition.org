#+title: Vector Addition
If two [[id:4180700c-adde-43ae-9fef-251975521d8e][Vectors]] are of the same \(R^n\) then they can be added
component wise.  If \(u = \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} \) and
\(v = \begin{pmatrix} -1 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}\) then \(u + v
= \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix} 
\)
