#+title: consistency of matrix equations

Example

If

\(A = \begin{pmatrix} 2 & 3 & 1 \\ -2 & -1 & 0 \\ 5 & 8 & 2
\end{pmatrix}, x = \begin{pmatrix} x_1 \\ x_2 \\ x_3 \end{pmatrix}\)
and b = \(\begin{pmatrix}
0 \\ 0 \\ 0
\end{pmatrix}\) then write \(Ax = b\) as a vector

\(x = x_1\begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ -2 \\ 4 \end{pmatrix} +
x_2 \begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ -1 \\ 8 \end{pmatrix} + x_3 \begin{pmatrix} 1
\\ 0 \\ 2 \end{pmatrix}\)
