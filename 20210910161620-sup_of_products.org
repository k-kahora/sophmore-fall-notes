#+title: Sum of Products

- A Boolean expression consisting of each term of a function expressed with AND operators, with all terms joined by OR operators
- Example: The [[id:6614b990-be11-45f9-8b15-27ec6467d4a9][canonical equation]] of F = a'bc + ab'c + a'b'c' is
  a SoP expression
