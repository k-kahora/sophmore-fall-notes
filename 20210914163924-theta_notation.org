#+title: theta notation

* Formal definition
- \(f(n) = \Theta(g(n)) \) if there are positive constants \(n_0\), \(c_1\), and
  \(c_2\) such that to the right of \(n_0\), the value of \(f(n)\)
  always between \(c_1*g(n)\) and \(c_2 * g(n) \) inclusive
