#+title: Existence and Uniqueness Theorem


This theroum states that a linear system
is[[ id:9221f5ea-b6fc-46f9-9bf9-a729ac0a658a][ consistent]] if and only if
the right most column of the [[id:08dce69d-0252-4201-9f50-e864901fd373][matrix]] in [[id:b9f83973-b253-4dde-a930-6bbfe0b6846a][augmented matrix]] form has no
row of the form

\(\begin{bmatrix} 0 & ... & 0 & b \end{bmatrix}\)

with b as non zero
